Welcome to Online Exam System

Online Exam System is a web-based platform that helps students take exams online in an easy and secure way. Students can log in, select their subjects, and attempt tests from anywhere. The system automatically evaluates objective questions and provides instant results.

The platform also offers study materials, exam schedules,and performance reports. Students can track their progress and identify areas where they need improvement.
